Logistical Regression

A Logistic regression is a classification algorithm used to predict a discrete set of classes or categories.

In this instance, we are training the model to predict if a person makes greater than $50K or less than $50K annually. Our logistic regression model had a training score of 79.39%. The test data has a score of 80.73%. As a result, the logistic regression test had an accuracy of 80.73%.

K Nearest Neighbor

A K Nearest Neighbor algorithm assumes the similarity between the new case/data and available cases and put the new data into the category that is most similar to the available categories.

In this instance, we are training the model to predict if a person makes greater than $50K or less than $50K annually. Our KNN model had a training score of 85.00%. The test data has a score of 83.33%. As a result, the KNN test has an accuracy of 83.33%
